# KPI-Performance-Dashboard---Business-Decision-Support
## Project Overview ##
This Power BI project delivers a client‑ready KPI Performance Dashboard designed to support executive decision‑making.
The solution focuses on monitoring revenue, costs, profit, and performance versus targets, enabling management to quickly identify deviations, understand key drivers, and take corrective actions.
The dashboard follows a realistic business reporting approach, emphasizing clarity, consistency, and insight over visual complexity.

---

## Business Objectives ##
- Monitor core business KPIs in a single executive view
- Track actual performance versus monthly targets
- Identify revenue and cost variances
- Analyze performance by product and region
- Support data‑driven operational and financial decisions

---

## Dashboard Structure ##
### 1. Executive KPI Overview ###
High‑level management view focused on current performance and target alignment.
- KPI cards for Revenue, Cost, Profit, Margin, and Variance
- Monthly revenue trends
- Revenue vs target comparison
- Revenue distribution by region

### 2. Performance Drilldown ###
Deeper analysis of business performance drivers.
- Revenue, profit, and margin analysis by product
- Revenue vs profit relationship using scatter analysis
- Regional margin comparison
- Interactive filtering for focused insights

### 3. Variance & Root Cause Analysis ###
Explains why performance deviates from plan.
- Revenue and cost variance KPIs
- Revenue variance by region
- Cost variance by product
- Decomposition Tree for structured root cause analysis

---

## Data Model ##
- Star schema design
- Fact tables: Sales, Costs, Targets
- Dimension tables: Date, Product, Region
- Monthly targets handled via DAX to ensure accurate context and scalable analytics

---

## Tools & Techniques
- Power BI Desktop
- Power Query (data preparation and transformation)
- DAX (KPIs, variance analysis, profit and margin calculations)
- Star schema modeling
- Decomposition Tree for root‑cause analysis
- Executive‑oriented dashboard design
